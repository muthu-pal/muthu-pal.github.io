about me


Hi, I'm Muthu Palaniappan. I'm currently a sophomore at UCLA studing Computer Science. I'm a developer and designer.


I use technology in every aspect of my life, from taking lecture notes on my tablet, to ordering iced coffee on the Philz app, and to checking my blood glucose levels on my phone. Seeing how big a role technology has played in my life, my goal is to create products that stimulate productivity and simplify everyday life.
